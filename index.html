Shakespear was right about love;
I’ll love you always;
I’ll love you all ways;
Like the sun it will forever burn;
Like the moon it will forever shine;
But even though yours is not mine;
Mine will never cease to die;
How I wish for you to be mine;
For you turned me into a beggar;
And you the thief;
For you stole the breath, from my lungs;
Stole the thoughts, from my mind;
Stole the heart, from my chest;
And all I beg from you;
Is to steal the love from me;
But all that is the sensation of mind
